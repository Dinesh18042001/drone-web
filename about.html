At Drone Works Consultancy (DWC), we are a team of sea- soned drone operators who have specialised in drone technology since 2011. We have established ourselves as a full- service provider in the ever-expanding unmanned aviation industry, focusing on data acquisition through 2D/3D aerial mapping and surveying for commercial and industrial sec- tors. We also create high-quality media content for film and cinema production

With many years of project experience and a diverse fleet of drones equipped with the latest sensors, we offer a wide range of drone services across Fiji, including Vitilevu and Vanualevu. Our pilots are highly qualified, licensed, and experienced in professional drone deployment. At Drone Works Consultancy, safety, precision, and customer satisfaction are our top priorities.

Why Choose Drone Works Consultancy?

Experience Since 2011 : Our experienced pilots bring over a decade of expertise in the UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) field.

Flexibility : Our infrastructure allows for quick, on-demand drone deployments to meet tight project dead- lines.

Large UAV Fleet :We own a wide range of drones, not re- stricted to any particular manufacturer, ensuring we al- ways use the best technology for each project.

Full-Service Project Management : From start to finish, we provide personalised, reliable and efficient project, management to ensure seamless execution.

Licensed Across Fiji : Our pilots are fully licensed and ex- perienced with local and international projects in ae- rial mapping, surveying, and media production across Fiji, Vanuatu, and other Pacific Islands.

Standardised Data Processing : We follow stan- dardised processes for pre- and post-data processing to ensure fast and reliable transmission.

Expert Evaluation : Our team of engineers, geographers, and registered surveyors guarantees accurate data pro- cessing and evaluation.

Our Commitment to Safety and Security

At Drone Works Consultancy, safety is our top priority. We use contact-free data collection from the air, eliminating the need for anyone to enter dangerous areas. The data col- lected is securely processed and evaluated by our back-office staff, ensuring complete privacy and protection.

What Makes Us Different?

Independent of UAV Manufacturers: We deploy project-specific UAVs based on the latest technology without being restricted to any particular brand.

Advanced Sensors :We use UAVs equipped with LiDAR scanners, infrared sensors, and multispectral sen- sors to deliver the most accurate results

Redundant Systems for Safety : Our drones have re- dundant systems on-site, ensuring continuous operation even if a system component fails.

Large Area Coverage : We can capture areas of up to 100 hectares per flight with quick battery changes, ensur- ing efficient coverage.

100% Autonomous : Our operations are fully autono- mous, utilizing advanced flight control systems and a power generator for seamless fieldwork.

High-Resolution Aerial Images and Videos : We cap- ture images with up to 42-megapixel resolution and videos in up to 7K quality, ideal for both commercial use and cinematic productions.

GNSS RTK Receivers : Our latest GNSS RTK receivers gather ground control point data with high precision, en- suring top-tier quality control during field operations.
