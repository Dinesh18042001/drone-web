Drone technology, state-of-the-art sensors and continuously improved software for evaluation create almost limitless opportunities. 3D data gathering and capturing of media content from the air enable companies and organisations to take wholly new paths and to design new processes. Yet the application and establishment of drone-assisted data logging and evaluation bring more challenges and hurdles with them than some of the marketing videos from big manufacturers would have you believe. At least if you are looking for a comprehensive and long-lasting solution for a business. In the field of drone consulting, we act as an innovation partner and help businesses be a step ahead of the tough competition.

The rapid change in the emergent drone market is enormous. This applies to both the hardware and software and also the legal frameworks. We have our drone fleet in use every day with different sensors and/or cameras in order to successfully complete highly diverse tasks. As a professional drone service provider, it is very important to us always to be a pioneer in the field of drones and sensor technology. We are independent and therefore very open in all areas. This makes us agile, maintaining our capacity to implement the latest technologies in the shortest period of time. Benefit from our experience, projects and innumerable tests. We advise customers in a focused, competent and strategic manner. This conserves resources and saves on capital and valuable time spent on implementing and using drone technology.

INTRODUCTION TO AND CONSULTING REGARDING DRONE-ASSISTED INFORMATION AND DATA LOGGING

Want to benefit from the latest technological developments and efficient drone-assisted information and data gathering but finding the subject matter inaccessible due to its complexity? We know the problem! Tell us how and where you want to deploy UAVs (drones) in your business or project and we will advise you based on your intentions. We pick you up at your current position and provide you with supplementary information to give you a solid foundation for further decision-making. Furthermore, we advise you strategically based on your assignment(s). Subject areas here could be drone models, UAV cameras/sensors and their technical options, advantages as well as limits. Other important subjects for getting involved in drone-assisted information and data recording are the legal bases governing the commercial use of UAVs, privacy rights and data protection.

PROOF OF CONCEPT: PLANNING AND IMPLEMENTING PILOT PROJECTS

It is already clear that productivity and efficiency in information and data logging can be increased considerably through the strategic use of drones. Many businesses, organisations and private individuals therefore have greater interest in integrating UAVs into their everyday. For many “simple” applications in the usual environments, this is certainly not a big problem once the right hardware has been chosen. For complex tasks, requirements at the limits of UAV technology, sensors or the pilot, as well operation in unusual environments, it is worth investing in a carefully planned pilot project with professional support. We perform pilot projects for the “proof of concept” using your specifications and the desired output. A drone pilot project usually saves valuable time which has to be invested in a number of internal tests without adequate theoretical and/or practical know-how. Furthermore, the use of drones to the limits can be very dangerous and costly. In terms of costs, you also save with a practical proof of concept in other respects in that you do not need to keep any staff do not usually have to invest in your own equipment and the training of pilots.

IMPLEMENTATION OF DRONE TECHNOLOGY IN ORGANISATIONS, COMPANIES OR PROJECTS

The implementation and commissioning of new technology involve various challenges. Often the focus here is on the hardware and software and not on the staff who will later be working every day with the technology and the results produced from it. We see the implementation of drone technology in organisations, companies and projects as a holistic matter in which both hardware and personnel play important roles. We advise you based on our experience in relation to implementation of drone technology which is as seamless as possible and accompany you on this journey. In addition, upon request, we will support you with information events as a speaker regarding the use of drone technology or train your staff in sessions and workshops.

WORKFLOW AND PROCESS CONSULTING REGARDING UAV INVENTORY SOLUTIONS AND ASSET MANAGEMENT

The introduction, establishment and optimisation of processes is difficult for many companies. Often external stimuli are required for this purpose. This is where we come in and advise you regarding sensible and effective workflows. This also includes advice regarding work instructions with strict compliance with permits and (external/internal) regulations, as well as consulting relating to checklists and logbooks.

INDEPENDENT DRONE HARDWARE CONSULTING

Drone Works Consultancy is totally manufacturer-independent and uses drone appropriate to the task in hand. This makes us free and keeps us flexible. That is why we operate different models from various drone manufacturers. We are very well connected within the drone industry and always at the cutting edge in relation to drone hardware. We are able to distinguish products overloaded with marketing promises from products that function in practice and test them for ourselves or on your behalf in the event of any doubt. We will advise you with the necessary expertise in line with your requirements, areas of application and situations so that you find the right drone hardware for yourself, your business or organisation. Subsequently we will also advise you, if so desired, regarding purchasing in terms of the right scope of the package(s); i.e. the quantity of batteries, accessories, spare and wear parts, etc.

CONSULTING REGARDING DRONE PAYLOADS

Experts such as ourselves, who work with different drone payloads from various manufacturers on a daily basis, best understand their strengths, weaknesses and limits. We work exclusively in an application-related manner in order to be able to achieve the best possible results for our service customers. The right choice of drone payload is not decisive in terms of the logging, but instead has an impact on the scope of data evaluation and thus the ROI. We advise on manufacturers independently and always within the context of your requirements, budgets or projects.

TAILORED DRONE SOLUTIONS

Looking for a tailored drone solution, recording or transport option via drone for your business? We are the right partner for developing a bespoke drone solution with you. Together with you, we will work through the requirements to the finished product and cater for your desires or project-specific parameters during this process. We have the relevant specialists for planning, designing and producing prototypes, small series, adapters and special solutions in house.

DRONE TRAINING AND DRONE WORKSHOPS

With us you can experience drone technology up close and benefit from practical training – irrespective of whether you are a newbie, already a drone pilot or already have specific questions from your field of application. We offer exclusively private training sessions and workshops. After all, this is the only way we can adapt each training session to your requirements. In this process, for example, we will explain the technically correct use of drone hardware and give classes on project planning, implementation and evaluation using software in the back office. We can also offer the “knowledge certificate” for unmanned flying devices (also referred to colloquially as a “drone driving licence”) through one of our partners.

TRAINING ON THE JOB

Need support with your work or a project directly on site? Experience our workflow with our “Training on the Job” training offer. Learn our tips and tricks on recording. This will give you critical advantages for project implementation, especially on future projects. We will show you how to record your object or building completely and effectively when you have the necessary equipment and framework conditions.

PHOTOGRAMMETRY SOFTWARE CONSULTING AND SOFTWARE TRAINING

There are currently different photogrammetry software products from large and small manufacturers on the market. Most of the software solutions have a right to be there, but it is often difficult to find the right solution for your own business which is consistent with the existing hardware and software environment. Unnecessarily high costs can be avoided in advance through strategic advice. In addition to consulting, we also offer software training for various manufacturers. This includes not only software on photogrammetric evaluation, but also flight planning.
